Olumo Rock

Olumo Rock famed as the founding site of Abeokuta, Nigeria. A massive outcrop of granite rock that towers above the city, the rock served as a natural fortress for the Egba people during the 19th century. It offered a great vantage point during the tribal wars and helped the locals win the wars.
